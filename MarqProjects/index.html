Landing Pages

Visit my blog marquinamarie.com to learn how to use landing pages to grow your business.
Landing Page Builders: Unbounce, Lander, PageWiz, LeadPages, Instapage.

Why use landing pages? The goal of landing pages is to convince the visitor to provide you with his or her information in exchange for a high-value offer.

Traditionally (or technically), a landing page refers to the first page a new visitor sees on your website, usually arriving via an ad or search engine. Specifically for books, we’re expanding on that definition a bit. A book landing page is a single permanent page on an author website or blog which contains all the relevant information for a given book, including a link to purchase. For example, an author landing page URL would follow a structure similar to this: http://www.YourAuthorSite.com/your-book-title
